# Image Recommendations for LiaiZen Landing Page

Based on Reddit/Facebook research into high-conflict co-parenting pain points, here are images that will emotionally resonate with your audience.

---

## üéØ KEY PRINCIPLE: Show Relief, Not Perfection

Your audience doesn't want to see:
- ‚ùå Perfect, smiling co-parents being best friends
- ‚ùå Corporate stock photos of "diverse teams"
- ‚ùå Overly staged "happy divorced family"

They WANT to see:
- ‚úÖ Real moments of relief/peace
- ‚úÖ Protection from chaos
- ‚úÖ Visual metaphors for "buffer" and "breathing room"
- ‚úÖ Authentic emotional states (tired ‚Üí calm, stressed ‚Üí peaceful)

## üìç HERO SECTION (Currently has placeholder shield icon)

### Option 1: "The Exhale" Moment
**Image:** Close-up of parent's face, eyes closed, deep breath, phone in hand but screen down
- **Emotion:** Relief, peace, centering
- **Message:** "This is what it feels like to have LiaiZen"
- **Style:** Natural lighting, authentic, not posed
- **Diversity:** Rotate through different genders, ethnicities, ages

### Option 2: Split Screen Transformation
**Image:** Left: stressed parent staring at phone (tension in face) ‚Üí Right: same parent, relaxed, looking away from phone
- **Emotion:** Before/after, hope, change
- **Message:** "LiaiZen creates the space between trigger and response"
- **Visual cue:** Maybe subtle glow/filter between the two images

### Option 3: Abstract Protection Visual
**Image:** Simple illustration of message bubbles with a soft filter/shield between them
- **Emotion:** Protection, mediation, buffer
- **Message:** "AI that sits between you and the chaos"
- **Style:** Modern, minimalist, calming colors (teal/white)

### Option 4: Parent + Child (Not Looking at Phone)
**Image:** Parent and child together (reading, cooking, playing), phone visible but face-down/ignored
- **Emotion:** Present parenting, peace, priorities
- **Message:** "Get your focus back where it belongs"
- **Key:** Phone is in frame but NOT the focus

## üé® OVERALL VISUAL STYLE GUIDELINES

### Color Palette (aligned with your brand)
- **Primary:** Teal (#4DA8B0) - calm, trust, mediation
- **Secondary:** Deep teal (#275559) - stability, protection
- **Accent:** Soft mint (#E6F7F5) - peace, breathing room
- **Warning:** Amber (for "problem" sections) - not red (too aggressive)

### Photography Style
- **Lighting:** Natural, soft (not harsh fluorescent)
- **Mood:** Calm, hopeful, authentic (not staged/cheesy)
- **Focus:** Emotional states, not objects
- **People:** Real-looking (not model-perfect)

### Illustration Style (if using illustrations)
- **Type:** Line art or simple flat design
- **Mood:** Warm, approachable, modern
- **Avoid:** Cartoon-y, childish, or corporate-stiff

## üö´ IMAGES TO AVOID

‚ùå **Angry/yelling parents** (reinforces negativity, doesn't show solution)

## üìê IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### File Requirements
- **Format:** WebP (with JPG fallback)
- **Size:** Optimized for web (< 200KB per image)
- **Dimensions:**
  - Hero: 1200x800px minimum
  - Section images: 800x600px minimum
  - Icons/illustrations: SVG preferred

### Accessibility
- All images should have descriptive alt text
- Don't rely on image alone to convey critical information
- Maintain text contrast even over images

## üé¨ WHERE TO SOURCE IMAGES

### Stock Photo Sites (Search Terms)
- **Unsplash/Pexels:** "single parent peaceful", "phone anxiety relief", "divorced parent calm"
- **Avoid generic:** "happy family", "business people", "success"
- **Try:** "exhausted parent", "phone stress", "deep breath", "moment of peace"

### Illustration Sources
- **Undraw.co** - Customizable color SVG illustrations
- **Humaaans** - Mix-and-match character illustrations
- **Storyset** - Animated illustration scenes

### Custom Creation
- Hire illustrator on Fiverr/Upwork for custom "buffer" visuals
- Commission photographer for authentic "relief" moments
- Consider AI-generated (DALL-E, Midjourney) for metaphorical images
